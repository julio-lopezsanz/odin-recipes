Meatballs

Ingredients

  • Ground Meat
  • Seasonings and Herbs
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Water
  • Italian Bread/li>

Steps

  1. Combine the first five ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the bread crumbs and slowly add water.
  3. shape the mixture into meatballs.
  4. Try the meatballs until they're all brown and crips.
